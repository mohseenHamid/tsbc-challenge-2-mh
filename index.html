About Me

Mohseen is an aspiring Software Engineer with interests in Energy & Sustainability, Agile, and Technology Consulting. Through his degree in Physics, and a host of extracurricular pursuits which include learning the Arabic language, Mohseen has developed strong problem-solving and critical analysis skills. He has led and overlooked multiple teams and projects as the president of a UCL society in 2018/19 and is presently a board member of a local community organisation. Mohseen’s desire to create impactful change led him to pivot to the world of technology and join Accenture. He is a member of the UKI SN team, within the Community & Networking pillar, working to map out and demystify Accenture’s sustainability landscape for analysts. Mohseen is keen for BA and Application Development roles that will grow his skills across the technology landscape, while also furthering his functional skillset.
